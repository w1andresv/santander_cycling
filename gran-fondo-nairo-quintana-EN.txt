ITINERARY
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

DAY 1 — WELCOME TO CARTAGENA
-------------------------------
Our journey begins in the city of Cartagena, one of the most iconic cities in
the Colombian Caribbean, renowned for its rich history, colonial architecture,
and vibrant colors. Upon arrival, we will transfer to the hotel where we will
stay throughout the tour.

After check-in and room assignments, we will prepare the bicycles for our
adventure in this beautiful city.

In the afternoon, we will gather for a welcome coffee to get to know each other
better and to talk about the tour, the itinerary, and general recommendations
for the upcoming days.

In the evening, we will enjoy dinner at the hotel, allowing time to rest after
the journey and recharge before beginning this great experience.


DAY 2 — HISTORIC CENTER OF CARTAGENA
--------------------------------------
Our first cycling experience will take place along a smooth and scenic route,
riding the coastal road toward the city of Barranquilla, known as "The Golden
Gate of Colombia." This relaxed ride will allow us to enjoy the Caribbean
landscapes as we prepare for the days ahead.

After lunch, we will set out to explore some of the city's most representative
landmarks, including the Castillo de San Felipe de Barajas, the Museo del Cacao
Cartagena, the iconic Monumento a los Zapatos Viejos, the Torre del Reloj, and
the Walled City of Cartagena.

To conclude the day, we will enjoy dinner in Getsemani, a lively neighborhood
known for its local cuisine, colorful streets, and vibrant atmosphere.

Today's Activities:
  - Coastal Highway Ride to Barranquilla
  - Discovering Cartagena's Historic Walled City

  [ STAGE 1 — Bocagrande to Arroyo Grande ]
  Distance : 82.13 km
  Elevation: 248 m


DAY 3 — CRYSTAL-CLEAR BEACHES
-------------------------------
Today will be dedicated to discovering crystal-clear beaches, where we will
enjoy a full day surrounded by paradise-like landscapes. We will travel to Baru
Beach and take a scenic tour of the Rosario Islands, with a stop at the Gran
Oceanario de Cartagena (swimming with dolphins is not included). At the end of
the tour, we will savor a delicious lunch featuring fresh sea fish, then return
to the hotel for dinner.

Today's Activities:
  - A Full Day Immersed in Crystal-Clear Caribbean Waters
  - Exploring Marine Life at the Cartagena Aquarium
  - Sailing Through the Breathtaking Rosario Islands


DAY 4 — HORSEBACK RIDING ALONG THE SHORE
------------------------------------------
We will enjoy a short cycling ride to explore more of Cartagena on two wheels.
After the ride, we will return to the hotel to get ready and wait for
transportation to take us to the beach, where we will enjoy a delicious lunch
and a horseback riding experience along the shoreline, while admiring the
sunset of Cartagena.

Today's Activities:
  - A Scenic Short Ride Through the Outskirts of Cartagena
  - A Magical Horseback Ride Along the Caribbean Shoreline

  [ STAGE 2 — Bocagrande to Arjona to Bocagrande ]
  Distance : 72.26 km
  Elevation: 508 m


DAY 5 — BAY TOUR
-----------------
We will start the day with a free morning, allowing you to relax and enjoy the
hotel. In the afternoon, we will take a short walk along the beach, where you
will find various handicrafts and have the opportunity to buy souvenirs.

To conclude the day, we will experience a tour of the bay, enjoying the sunset
and having dinner on board a boat with crew.

Today's Activities:
  - A Leisurely Morning at the Hotel
  - Discovering Bocagrande, Cartagena's Modern Coastline
  - Golden Hour Over Cartagena Bay


DAY 6 — WELCOME RIDE
----------------------
We will start the day with a welcome ride organized by the event. Afterward,
we will return to the hotel for lunch. In the afternoon, we will visit the
official Gran Fondo Nairo Quintana Trade Fair, where you can enjoy inspiring
talks, guest artists, commercial exhibitions, pick up your event kit, and
discover many more exciting surprises.

Later, we will return to the hotel to prepare our bicycles, meals, and make any
final adjustments for what awaits us on Sunday — an unforgettable day of
cycling and celebration.

  Event dates: October 9 to 11

Today's Activities:
  - Welcome Ride Ahead of the Gran Fondo
  - Gran Fondo Expo & Commercial Fair Afternoon
  - Official Event Kit Collection


DAY 7 — GRAN FONDO NAIRO QUINTANA
------------------------------------
The big day has arrived! We will experience a race alongside the great Nairo
Quintana and his special guests. You'll enjoy excellent hydration and plenty of
photo opportunities, so remember to always smile when you see a camera. You
will reach the finish line and be welcomed like a true professional, enjoying
an experience you will always remember. We will follow the closing ceremony
instructions to finish Sunday sharing this moment with all participants.

  Choose your challenge:

  GRAN FONDO  — 186 km | 1,150 m elevation gain
  "Want to go further and live a more complete experience? This one is for you!"

  MEDIO FONDO — 106 km | 550 m elevation gain
  "Want the perfect balance between challenge and enjoyment?"

  Route: Cartagena (Start & Finish) -> Santa Rosa de Lima -> Villanueva


DAY 8 — THANK YOU FOR CHOOSING US
------------------------------------
Our experience has come to an end. We hope you enjoyed every moment with us,
that you loved the local cuisine, and that the memories you take with you will
last forever and be shared with your loved ones. We look forward to seeing you
again and being part of your great cycling adventures in Colombia.

We will be ready to take you to the airport so you can return home safely.
