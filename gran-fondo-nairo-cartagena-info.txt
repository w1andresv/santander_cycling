================================================================
  ITINERARIO
================================================================

----------------------------------------------------------------
  DAY 1 — Welcome to Cartagena
  Aeropuerto Internacional Rafael Núñez
----------------------------------------------------------------
Our journey begins in the city of Cartagena, one of the most
iconic cities in the Colombian Caribbean, renowned for its rich
history, colonial architecture, and vibrant colors. Upon arrival,
we will transfer to the hotel where we will stay throughout the
tour.

After check-in and room assignments, we will prepare the
bicycles for our adventure in this beautiful city.

In the afternoon, we will gather for a welcome coffee to get
to know each other better and to talk about the tour, the
itinerary, and general recommendations for the upcoming days.

In the evening, we will enjoy dinner at the hotel, allowing
time to rest after the journey and recharge before beginning
this great experience.

  Highlights:
  - Transfer al hotel boutique
  - Preparación de bicicletas
  - Welcome coffee y presentación del tour
  - Cena en el hotel

----------------------------------------------------------------
  DAY 2 — Historic Center of Cartagena
  Ruta costera + Recorrido cultural
----------------------------------------------------------------
Our first cycling experience will take place along a smooth
and scenic route, riding the coastal road toward the city of
Barranquilla, known as "The Golden Gate of Colombia." This
relaxed ride will allow us to enjoy the Caribbean landscapes
as we prepare for the days ahead.

After lunch, we will set out to explore some of the city's
most representative landmarks.

To conclude the day, we will enjoy dinner in Getsemaní, a
lively neighborhood known for its local cuisine, colorful
streets, and vibrant atmosphere.

  Highlights:
  - Rodada por la vía costera (ruta tranquila)
  - Castillo de San Felipe de Barajas
  - Museo del Cacao Cartagena
  - Monumento a los Zapatos Viejos
  - Torre del Reloj
  - Ciudad Amurallada de Cartagena
  - Cena en el barrio Getsemaní

----------------------------------------------------------------
  DAY 3 — Crystal-Clear Beaches
  Playa Barú + Islas del Rosario
----------------------------------------------------------------
Today will be dedicated to discovering crystal-clear beaches,
where we will enjoy a full day surrounded by paradise-like
landscapes. We will travel to Barú Beach and take a scenic
tour of the Rosario Islands, with a stop at the Gran
Oceanario de Cartagena.

* Swimming with dolphins is NOT included.

At the end of the tour, we will savor a delicious lunch
featuring fresh sea fish, then return to the hotel for dinner.

  Highlights:
  - Playa Barú
  - Tour por las Islas del Rosario
  - Gran Oceanario de Cartagena
  - Almuerzo con pescado fresco del mar
  - Regreso al hotel para cena

----------------------------------------------------------------
  DAY 4 — Horseback Riding Along the Shore
  Rodada + Playa + Atardecer
----------------------------------------------------------------
We will enjoy a short cycling ride to explore more of
Cartagena on two wheels. After the ride, we will return to
the hotel to get ready and wait for transportation to take us
to the beach, where we will enjoy a delicious lunch and a
horseback riding experience along the shoreline, while
admiring the sunset of Cartagena.

  Highlights:
  - Rodada corta por Cartagena
  - Almuerzo en la playa
  - Cabalgata a lo largo de la orilla del mar
  - Atardecer en Cartagena

----------------------------------------------------------------
  DAY 5 — Bay Tour
  Mañana libre + Recorrido por la bahía
----------------------------------------------------------------
We will start the day with a free morning, allowing you to
relax and enjoy the hotel. In the afternoon, we will take a
short walk along the beach, where you will find various
handicrafts and have the opportunity to buy souvenirs.

To conclude the day, we will experience a tour of the bay,
enjoying the sunset and having dinner on board a boat with crew.

  Highlights:
  - Mañana libre en el hotel
  - Paseo por la playa y compra de artesanías
  - Tour por la bahía de Cartagena
  - Cena a bordo del barco al atardecer

----------------------------------------------------------------
  DAY 6 — Welcome Ride
  Gran Fondo Nairo Quintana Fest · Cartagena 2026
----------------------------------------------------------------
We will start the day with a welcome ride organized by the
event. Afterward, we will return to the hotel for lunch.

In the afternoon, we will visit the official Gran Fondo Nairo
Quintana Trade Fair, where you can enjoy inspiring talks,
guest artists, commercial exhibitions, and pick up your
event kit.

Later, we will return to the hotel to prepare our bicycles,
meals, and make any final adjustments for the big race day
on Sunday.

  Highlights:
  - Welcome Ride oficial del evento
  - Almuerzo en el hotel
  - Feria comercial Gran Fondo Nairo Quintana
  - Charlas inspiradoras y artistas invitados
  - Recogida del kit del evento
  - Preparación final de bicicletas

----------------------------------------------------------------
  DAY 7 — Gran Fondo Nairo Quintana
  ¡El gran día! Cartagena · Bolívar · 2026
----------------------------------------------------------------
The big day has arrived! We will experience a race alongside
the great Nairo Quintana and his special guests. You'll enjoy
excellent hydration and plenty of photo opportunities — so
remember to always smile when you see a camera.

You will reach the finish line and be welcomed like a true
professional, enjoying an experience you will always remember.
We will follow the closing ceremony instructions to finish
Sunday sharing this moment with all participants.

  Modalidades disponibles:
  - Gran Fondo  : 186 km | 1.150 m de desnivel
  - Medio Fondo :  106 km |   550 m de desnivel

  Highlights:
  - Carrera junto a Nairo Quintana y sus invitados especiales
  - Hidratación y asistencia durante toda la ruta
  - Cruce de la meta como un profesional
  - Ceremonia de cierre del evento

----------------------------------------------------------------
  DAY 8 — Thank You for Choosing Us
  Cartagena → Aeropuerto Rafael Núñez
----------------------------------------------------------------
Our experience has come to an end. We hope you enjoyed every
moment with us, that you loved the local cuisine, and that
the memories you take with you will last forever and be
shared with your loved ones.

We look forward to seeing you again and being part of your
great cycling adventures in Colombia.

We will be ready to take you to the airport so you can
return home safely.

  Highlights:
  - Desayuno incluido
  - Transfer al Aeropuerto Rafael Núñez
  - ¡Hasta la próxima aventura en Colombia!
